Digital Streaming On The Rise


by Bizploy


Posted on July 28, 2018 at 6:00 PM

Content and media consumption is on an exponential rise across the globe. Increasing feasibility in terms of available devices combined with the high internet speed and accessibility has given people plethora of options. From entertainment to information, social media to live broadcasting, everything is available in real time, anywhere. The rising popularity of digital platforms like Netflix , Amazon, Hotstar, etc is a marker of changing times and attitudes of people who are gradually drifting away from the conditioned pattern of content consumption. This in turn is challenging the traditional perception of television being the sole source of media.


With sports events to Royal weddings being live streamed, it is convenient to watch it all on the go, making "being glued to the TV" outdated. Due to IPL streaming during April-May and other major streamings, Hotstar and Jio-Play witnessed a sharp rise from 35% to 43% and 15% to 37% respectively. The audience is looking for more than the mainstream daily soap content and is more welcoming towards unconventional material. A sharp fall in Voot and steady rise in Amazon viewership could be attributed to this.


Due to this transition, a paradigm shift is observed in terms of how advertising operates in a global context. Shifting the base from the conventional marketing channels, marketers are increasing allocating major part of the budget in digital platforms. This wave is leading to a number of ways in which the process can be monetized, namely-


  1. Generating Revenue Through Subscription Model


  2. Major Advertising Shift resulting in revenue generation.


  3. Payment mechanism driving monetization through various options available like wallets, net banking, third-party portals, credit or debit card transactions. Depending on the trend, India is majorly a wallet centric section which implies that partnering with them and convert the audience to a subscription base is possible.


Digital streaming platforms have an extremely lucrative and promising future especially when multiple players are marking their online presence. TV is no longer the only platform to ensure a brand presence. Marketers, as well as the audiences, are seeing the potential success and synergy in resonating with the digital media.
